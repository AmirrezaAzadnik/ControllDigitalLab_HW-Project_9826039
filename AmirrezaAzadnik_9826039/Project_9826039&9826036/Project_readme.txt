Defender_project:in robot dar ebteda az makan gharagiri avalie be mokhtasat darvaze 
miravad va sepas mogheEat y tup ra donbal mikonad va mane az goal shodan tup mishavad.

Forward_project: in robot hamvare mogheEat tup ra donbal mikonad va zamani ke khataye fasele an 
kamtar az yek mizan kuchak delkah mishavad, mogheEat delkhah an ke an ra donbal mikonad az
mogheEat tup be mogheEat vasat darvaze harif taghir mikonad. in  control be vasile yek halghe while
va yek if anjam mishavad, ke agar tup az robot fasele begirad, dobare be donbal tup miravad
va in ravand tekrar mishavad.
(harkat be samt mogheEat ha ba estefade az tamrin 4 anjam mishavad)
control konande haye mored estefade : baraye control zavie az P va baraye harkat khati az 
PID estefade shode ast.
mazaya va no Avari : control zavie dar ebteda va harkat ruye khat rast bar asas fasele dar harkat khati
hamchenin algoritm mored estefade baraye goal kardan tup
